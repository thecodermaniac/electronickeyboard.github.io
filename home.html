Top 5 budget eletronic keybords you can't miss as a Begineer!

Well, given by the number of awesome electronic keyboards out there. It's very common for the newbies to get carried away easily by their astonising looks. Besides it's fundamental to choose the best suited for you as it will help you in your learning process. Like all other hobbies you needn't to waste huge amounts to get started. Just get a good one and you are good to go! So I handpicked "5" of the Begineer-friendly and Budget-friendly elecronic keyboards for you.

Casio-PX350

This 88 key elecronic keyboard is mainly designed for amateurs and professionals who want a fully featured keyboars that sounds great. Advanced sound engine and touch sesitivity makes it "stands-out" from other competitors.With Casio's AIR sound processing engiene, it creates rich , dimentional piano sounds down to sounds of dampers on strings.

Pros:-

  • Full size,fully weighted ,touch sensitive keys.
  • High quality audio samples and dynamic speakers for great sounds.
  • Large libraries of voices ,rhythms,effects and other features.
  • Ample memory for user settings,songs and samples.
  • Pitch Bend Wheel
  • Compatible with pedals and half-pedal functions.
  • USB,audio outputs, and other terminals for accessories.
  • 250 instument voices, 180 built-in rhythm, 128 levels of polyphony, 10 song user library and other effects.

Cons:-

  • Not all instrument voices are high quality.

Yamaha PSR-EW300

Designed for intermediate or advanced players who are ready to upgrade to a more advanced instrument. Begineers who are ready to invest more can surely look for this item. Thebulit-in Yamaha education system makes it easier to learn and play. This 76 keys wide keyboard is driven by Yamaha's high quality AWM stereo sampling.

Pros:-

  • Great range of tones ,rhythms styles,effects amd more.
  • High quality AWM sound engine
  • 48 levels of polyphony
  • Backlit LCD screen for easy navigation
  • USB to HOST connectivity for MIDI files, along with AUX IN, headphone, and pedal jacks
  • 574 instrument voices, 18 drums and effects, and 154 built-in songs for tremendous versatility and great sound in a portable keyboard.
  • 165 auto-accompaniment styles for real-time backing tracks.

Cons:-

  • Keys are not weighted

Casio CTK-3500

If you are looking to indulge in learning piano. Then this electronic keyboard is great for you. It's also compatible with Casio's popular "Chordana" app , thus making learning more easier.

Pros:-

  • Lightweigth,portable and can be battery powered
  • Great range of voices,sounds and effects.
  • Dance Music Mode for unique dance music and rhythm.
  • 400 instument voices, 100 built-in rhythm, 46 levels of polyphony and a wide variety of sounds and effects.
  • Pitch Bend Wheel
  • USB port and MIDI support

Cons:-

  • Keyboard isn't weighted.

Yamaha PSR-E373

This 61 keys beast is equipped with a touch-sensitive keyboard and an all-new tone generator that delivers an amazing array of high-quality Voices, the Yamaha PSRE-373 is perfect for learning or just having fun. You can enjoy a comprehensive library of 622 instrument Voices perfect for playing in any genre you choose, as well as unique Super Articulation Lite Voices that reproduce the subtle nuances of stringed instruments

Pros:-

  • Packed with over 622 stunning sounding instrument Voices and a wealth of accompaniment Styles
  • Smart Chord lets you play great-sounding chords from simple Pop triads to complex jazz 7th chords with just a few fingers.
  • The USB to HOST port allows for both MIDI and audio to pass to and from a computer's recording software with a single cable, making the PSR-E373 a great instrument to get started with home recording.
  • “Smart Chord,” which lets you play lush sounding chords with just one finger, or “Multi,” which lets you play chords using normal fingering.
  • "Super Articulation Lite" Voices reproduce the unique nuances, heard when playing specific acoustic instruments, that are typically not possible to achieve on keyboard instruments.

Cons:-

  • Power adapter sold separately :(

  • Jam & Create your own musical composition by using the Dance Music Mode. Further add build-up effects to boost your performance
